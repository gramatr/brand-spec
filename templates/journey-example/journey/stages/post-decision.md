# Stage — Post-decision

The post-decision stage is where most vendor content becomes thin.
Marketing teams optimize for top-of-funnel; customer-success teams
optimize for ticket deflection; nobody optimizes for the prospect's
"I need to defend this in board prep" moment. That is what this
stage's content goal exists to address.

## Authorship notes

The post-decision stage is the easiest one to populate with verbatim
prospect language because existing customers will tell you exactly
what they need from you to defend the purchase internally. Mining QBRs
and renewal conversations for the literal phrases champions use is
faster than running net-new prospect interviews here.
