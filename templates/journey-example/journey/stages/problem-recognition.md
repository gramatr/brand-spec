# Stage — Problem recognition

The mid-market HR leader is in a "something is off" moment. Their
exec team has started asking pointed questions; the HR leader has a
dashboard that shows part of the story but cannot yet confidently say
whether the trend is signal or noise. They are searching for benchmarks
and diagnostic frameworks, not vendors.

## Authorship notes

The `triggers`, `hurdles`, `mental_vocabulary`, and `questions_asked`
above are illustrative for this template. Replace them with verbatim
content from your own prospect interviews before treating the stage as
production-ready. The single biggest failure mode for this layer is
inventing prospect language from the marketing team's intuition; the
output reads as on-brand and tests poorly because real prospects use
different words.
